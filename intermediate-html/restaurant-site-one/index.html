Papardella is the culmination of Tom and Jerry’s long held dream to open a pasta bar serving fresh, hand rolled pasta with delicious sauces and fillings inspired by their trips to Italy. Choose from a variety of pasta shapes, including spaghetti, fettuccine, and gnocchi, all made by hand daily. Our sauces range from classic tomato and basil to more adventurous options like truffle cream and wild mushroom. We also offer a range of delicious fillings, such as spinach and ricotta or slow-cooked beef ragù.
Our menu is designed to cater to all tastes and dietary requirements, with gluten-free and vegetarian options available. Pair your pasta with a carefully curated wine selection from Italy or a refreshing cocktail.
