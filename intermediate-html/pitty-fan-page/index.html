Pitty Fan Webpage

Priscilla Novaes Leone (born October 7, 1977, in Salvador, Bahia), better known as Pitty, is a rock singer. She was the most awarded Brazilian rock artist of the 2000s; including the MTV Video Music Brazil, Artist of the Year twice, Video of the Year, Best Live Performance, and three times as the lead singer of the Dream Band.

Visit the official website or a Wikipedia article about her.
