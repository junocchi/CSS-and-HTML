London, UK

Location

London stands on the River Thames in south-east England at the head of a 50-mile (80 km) estuary down to the North Sea, and has been a major settlement for two millennia.

About

Weather

Modern London has the equable climate of South East England, with mild winters and temperate summers. The average daytime air temperature is 52 °F (11 °C), with 42 °F (5.5 °C) in January and 65 °F (18 °C) in July. Statistics show that the sun shines, however briefly, on five days out of six.

Population

London's 2023 population is now estimated at 9,648,110.

Sports

London's most popular sport is football (soccer) and it has 12 Football League clubs including Arsenal, the most loved one.
